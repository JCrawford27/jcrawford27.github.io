Citrus Crunch

    Ingredients
  • 1/4cup Orange Juice
  • 1-2cups Water
  • 2 Green Apples
  • 1 Can of Pineapples
  • (Optional) 1 Protein Packet (Can be found in our merch tab.)
Fill your pitcher with 1-2 cups of water, 1/4cup of orange juice and your can of pineapples.

Juice your green apples. Add your "apple" juice to the mix. Empty your protein packet and thoroughly stir.

Orange you glad this Citrusy drink tastes absolutely wonderful; and also adds a surprise crunch (the pineapple).


Peachy Pear

    Ingredients
  • 1/4cup Pear Juice
  • 1-2cups Water
  • 4 Peaches
  • (Optional) 1cup Pomegranate Juice
Fill your pitcher with 1-2 cups of water, 1/4cup of pear juice and your can of pineapples.

Juice your peaches. Add your "peach" juice AND Pomegranate Juice to the mix and thoroughly stir.

You will a-peach-iate this peachy drink. Even without the succulent pear juice, this drink will really have you begging for another round!
