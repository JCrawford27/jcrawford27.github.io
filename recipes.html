Citrus Crunch

    Ingredients
  • 1/4cup Orange Juice
  • 1-2cups Water
  • 2 Green Apples
  • 1 Can of Pineapples
  • (Optional) 1 Protein Packet (Can be found in our merch tab.)
Fill your pitcher with 1-2 cups of water, 1/4cup of orange juice and your can of pineapples.

Juice your green apples. Add your "apple" juice to the mix. Empty your protein packet and thoroughly stir.

Orange you glad this Citrusy drink tastes absolutely wonderful; and also adds a surprise crunch (the pineapple).

Peachy Pear

    Ingredients
  • 1/4cup Pear Juice
  • 1-2cups Water
  • 4 Peaches
  • (Optional) 1cup Pomegranate Juice
Fill your pitcher with 1-2 cups of water, 1/4cup of pear juice and your can of pineapples.

Juice your peaches. Add your "peach" juice AND Pomegranate Juice to the mix and thoroughly stir.

You will a-peach-iate this peachy drink.

Sup' Strawberry

    Ingredients
  • 2cups of Strawberries
  • 1cup Water
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1 Sweet Packet (Can be found in our merch section.)
Fill your pitcher with 1 cup of water.

Juice your strawberries and lemon. Add your "strawberry-lemon" juice and Sweet Packet to the mix and thoroughly stir.

You will love this strawberry beverage berry much.

Killer Kiwi

    Ingredients
  • 3 Kiwis
  • 3cups of Water
  • 1cup of Strawberries
  • 1 Sweet Packet (Can be found in our merch section.)
Fill your pitcher with 3 cups of water.

Juice your kiwis and strawberries, then add the juice and the sweet packet into the mix. Thoroughly stir.

This drink holds the kiwis to your heart!
